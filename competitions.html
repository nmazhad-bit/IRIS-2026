Eligibility

  • All participants may consist of undergraduate students from any degree program in their universities with good standing at the time of application and remain in good standing until the end of the contest.
  • All participants can be from any degree program, with a preference for those majoring in Drilling and Petroleum Engineering.
  • Every team will be required to pay a registration fee of 200 MYR upon submitting the registration form.

Competition Format

Written Test: A 1.5-hour assessment with multiple-choice and short-answer questions covering key topics such as well control principles, drilling fluids, kick detection and response, well barriers, equipment, risk management, safety protocols, IADC standards, and case study analysis.
Simulation-Based Scenarios: A practical assessment where participants apply their knowledge using well control simulators to demonstrate hands-on skills in realistic scenarios.
